Witch

Woman with powers, gifts. Hooked nose, flies on a broom, uses a magic wand. A witch lives in a house. She practices witchcraft and magic.


Ghost

Human form dressed in a white sheet, floating above the ground. He drags chains behind him.


Vampire

A vampire lives by night: he cannot stand sunlight. He spends the day sleeping in a coffin, well hidden. A vampire has very long canines which are used to bite the necks of its victims, to drink their blood.


Werewolf

Half man, half wolf, he has a big mouth, sparkling eyes and sharp teeth. At each full moon, he transforms and becomes ferocious.


Mummy

Scary character surrounds strips of cloth


Zombie

Zombies look like bodies in various states of decomposition.They usually have a bluish complexion and scars all over their body.


Skeleton

Character who has no skin over the bones.


Demon

Scary character with horns.
